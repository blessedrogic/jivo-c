JivoChat Mobile SDK

a live chat in your mobile app without development costs

Use a pre-built solution to implement an additional communication channel in your Android or iOS app

What JivoChat Mobile SDK has to offer

A live chat in the app

The mobile app users do not have to go the site or messengers to contact you

A single window for all messages

Messages from the mobile app, as well as from other channels, go to the JivoChat app and are no longer lost

Chatbots

Chatbots respond to customers outside business hours and close up to 50% of request without staff

Push notification

The customer will see your respond even if the app is closed. JivoChat will notify about the chat message via mobile push

Customer satisfaction ratings

With statistics and customer feedback you can track the staff efficiency

Mobile SDK integration on white paper: for iOS and Android

JivoChat Mobile SDK for Carprice

Carprice develops technologies and infrastructure solutions for selling used cars through an online auction. The mobile app is the main product for dealers, and communication with them is the key.

Earlier the mobile app users couldn't contact us directly. We were losing leads because customers could hardly find how to contact us on WhatsApp, and some of them simply did not want to spend time on it.

With the JivoChat Mobile SDK, we began to receive requests directly from the mobile app. It has become easier and more convenient or customers to contact us. We, in turn, have significantly saved on the development of our own solution and reduced the time for its implementation.

JivoChat Mobile SDK is available in the JivoChat Enterprise version
